Phase 1

Phase 1 focussed on research solely with users, this was to help us to understand what, if any, are the needs and perceived benefits of {{ data['maintitle'] }}.

In order to do this, we focussed our research on a small, linear user journey.

Phase 2

Phase 2 introduced research with services.

Once we discovered that users deemed {{ data['maintitle'] }} to be of high benefit and value, we needed to understand the feasibility of joining up services and creating personalisation across government.

  1. We defined our objectives and key results as a team [link to]
  2. We defined our features backlog to test within research lead by our research findings within phase 1, the features backlog is made up of 33 features
  3. We decided on an approach that would best allow us to our features with end users and services
  4. Once our research concluded, we measured each feature on a matrix of user impact, value and complexity of adoption for services
  5. Finally, we used our research findings and matrix to prioritise features to do conduct a ‘deep dive’ in to within phase 3
