Life while having a baby

Phase 1 focussed on joining up services within a predictable, linear user journey.

Stages of life often happen simultaneously, for example, buying a larger home or needing to learn how to drive because you’re expecting a baby.

Therefore, phase 2 focused on cutting across multiple user journeys, allowing us to understand and test the complex scenarios and challenges of joining up services across government.

Life event criteria
  • A wide range of services and departments across government
  • Includes users that are easy to reach for user research
  • A diverse range of service users
  • Service owners that are easy to access and engage with
  • Has data that we are able to access
  • Supports the features we want to test
  • Include a broad set of data
  • Spans a couple of years in a person’s life
