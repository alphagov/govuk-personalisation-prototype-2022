{{ data['recommendationheadings']['why'] }}

In phase 2 of our project, we found a wide variation in the usage of data dictionaries and adherence to the mandatory and recommended information standards.

We found that many services did not have a data dictionary, although services that were larger or had recently undergone a digital transformation were more likely to.

Having an accurate picture of the data items that services record and validate, and the identity profiles required for their users will enable greater understanding of the data needs of services across government.

{{ data['recommendationheadings']['what'] }}

  • Understanding of service data needs
  • Supporting the development of a cross-government data dictionary
