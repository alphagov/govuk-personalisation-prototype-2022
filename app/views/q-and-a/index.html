Which service team groups feel like the main users of the GOV.UK Account?
(DI currently targeting teams who already do identity checks but these teams tend to follow existing departmental standards - is this the same for teams following data assurance standards?)
Should we be targeting new teams / teams without existing standards / well ingrained processes already in place ?

Based on learnings from this research, differences in data assurance standards across government presents challenges for the future.

For some services differences in standards between their service and other government services could make consuming data difficult - they may only be willing to be a source of data rather than a consumer of data.

For these services they may not see the benefit of participating in data sharing activity within the context of a GOV.UK account.
