How sharing permissions work

Sharing information

  • Select ‘Once’ to share the requested information on this application only.
  • Select ‘Always’ to allow the person requesting to use your information on any applications. You will be notified any time your information is used. You can revoke access at any time in your account.

Information visibility

  • Select ‘Show details’ to allow the person requesting to see your information.
  • Select ‘Hide details’ to hide your information from the person requesting and only share with the application.
