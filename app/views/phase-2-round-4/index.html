Phase 2 / Round 4

To walkthrough this prototype, follow the tasks below (suggestion: keep them open on a separate tab).


Tasks

  1. Start on GOV.UK homepage
  2. View GOV.UK account explained page
  3. Click 'Sign in or create a GOV.UK account' link & sign in
  4. Review account dashboard
  5. Prove Your Identity
  6. add employment details
  7. Final ID checks
  8. Prove your ID successful
  9. Back to account - review updates incl. trusted data items
  10. Request to Share data
  11. Link account confirmation page
  12. Choose if and how you share data
  13. Return to account
  14. Review audit trail

Objectives

  1. Understand if there is an appetite to prove ID as part of GOV.UK account set up (continuation from round 3, there is a content iteration here)
  2. Explore user comprehension of what Proving your Identity enables (continued from round 3)
  3. Explore user comprehension of trusted data items (continued from rounds 1-3)
  4. Find out whether users are willing to share their data with their partner, as part of a joint transaction (in this case, getting help paying for childcare)
  5. Understand the perceived pros and cons to shared data.
  6. Explore whether an audit log is understood and whether it enhances trust and confidence

Hypotheses / assumptions

  • We believe that users will express willingness to share data as long as there is a clear benefit to doing so
  • We believe that users will appreciate the controls around information sharing (duration and visibility)
  • We believe that users will appreciate control and management of information sharing including the ability to stop sharing.
  • We believe that users will appreciate the transparency
