Objectives

  2. Explore user comprehension of what Proving your Identity enables (continued from round 3)
  3. Explore user comprehension of trusted data items (continued from rounds 1-3)
  4. Find out whether users are willing to share their data with their partner, as part of a joint transaction (in this case, getting help paying for childcare)

  6. Explore whether an audit log is understood and whether it enhances trust and confidence
