Services mostly reacted very positively to this feature and gave lots of examples of use cases for each of their services. Services spoke about whether documents would be automatically saved or whether the end user would have the ability to upload documents. Services felt it was counter-intuitive to provide information to a person and then make the user upload it back to the GOV.UK account.

"Depends on what you mean by documents, the ability to save scans of paper credentials, the ability to issue back a verifiable credential, an NHS login lookup so someone could see someone has LPA for someone or for a bank."

Services expanded on the idea of saved documents and spoke of the value of having the ability to have a 'saved status' which could be shared e.g. in receipt of PIP, have a Blue Badge.

Service use case

  • "Working with care leavers, they would often lose documentation, this would be really useful for the council." Camden LA
  • "It could be helpful to streamline the process - would need to connect to other services that are uploading evidence to their portal." DWP
