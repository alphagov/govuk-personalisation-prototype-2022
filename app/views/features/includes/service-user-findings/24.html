Services consistently saw value in this feature - particularly as a mechanism to inform users about renewing something. Services also felt that this feature could also have a positive impact on call volumes - by reminding people in advance that something is expiring, could result in reduced calls from people urgently wanting to renew. "We have a 14% drop off rate" [PIP reference to applicants not completing the application in time]

"Anytime we contact the citizen they aren't comfortable with a surprise call"

However, user engagement was a point of concern for services - There needs to be consideration about how we get people to engage with this feature - "I don't know if people would sign up just to be nudged by the government"

There is also some concern about ensuring reminders and the information within them are kept up to date.
