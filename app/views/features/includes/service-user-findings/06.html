This would be hugely valuable for services. At present, services have to implement data sharing collaborations with multiple government departments to gain access to verified data. In some cases they even have to buy in data or pay external organisations to verify user data.

Although it is seen as a hugely important feature, there are a lot of risks that need to be considered:

  • verified data needs to be controlled by the individual within a personal data store
  • many people wouldn't know how to manage their data and will look to get help from people
  • what does support access look like?
