Once users have signed into their GOV.UK account they'll be able to see their dashboard / account area.

In the short-term, this will allow them to:

  • view and manage their data
  • view recent account activity
  • manage their account details - such as their email address and password

In the future, the demands of the dashboard will increase once new features are introduced, warranting more controls and functionality.
