Within the GOV.UK account, there will be a dedicated section where users can manage their information at data item level.

Over time, for each data item, users may be able to:

  • Edit (this may depend on verified status)
  • Delete
  • Add
  • View an audit log
  • Share and revoke access to other GOV.UK account holders
  • Send/receive from an external data source
