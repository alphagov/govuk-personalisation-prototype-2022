We tested this feature as with a cross government scenario based on our 'childcare' life event. We believe that this feature was understood.

In our scenario, users were asked to consent to use information from their GOV.UK account before autofilling a transactional form. (On a related note, the consent prompt before using the data was received well as it builds confidence in how and why data is being used.)

Users then had the opportunity to check answers and edit fields before submitting transactional forms. In line with our design recommendations, users should always have control over final submissions.
