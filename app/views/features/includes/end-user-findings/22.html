User testing is limited in what it can tell us about navigation - there is no real-life task or transaction, and therefore the way in which user research participants navigate is influenced by the task we give them.

We did, however, gather some insights around navigation.

In our first round of testing, we used 'Get Childcare: step by step'. Research participants commented that there was a lot of information and we observed some users getting lost. In subsequent rounds, we opted to use a stripped-back version of the step-by-step.

A relevant finding through our work on curated content suggests that users will use curated information on the page to quickly scan for the information they need.
