In phase 2 we tested the concept of saving payment details to the GOV account. As part of a hypothetical recurring transaction, we also tested the idea of managing recurring payments from the GOV account.

The ability to connect details and manage payments received mixed reactions; whilst some users saw this as easy and convenient, others did not feel comfortable connecting financial details to a government account.

Some users commented that it was unclear why payment details would be required.

Users generally expected payment ‘method’ to be bank card details or direct debit. No one in our research sample mentioned Apple or Android pay as preferred methods.
