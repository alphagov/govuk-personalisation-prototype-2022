We used a variety of tags to indicate progress on transactional forms. These included 'in progress' 'complete' and 'urgent'.

In our testing scenarios, we placed these tags within the account area, and within the step-by-step navigation.

There were no remarkable reactions to progress indicators. The progress indicators were understood and received well by users.
