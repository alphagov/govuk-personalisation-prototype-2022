Users understood why some data might be fixed and others might be editable. Giving users the option to 'change' certain data items that are editable helped them understand which are editable and which aren't.

Examples from user research:

“For example, date of birth can’t change but names are things that change quite often.” from phase 2, round 1

“You can’t change your date of birth but you could move house.”from phase 2, round 1

“It looks like you can easily change or delete as well. For example, if I was no longer employed I could delete or change it here.” from phase 2, round 1
