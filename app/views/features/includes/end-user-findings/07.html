Notifications were understood and we believe they were positively received although user reactions were fairly unremarkable.

Some users commented that they would expect to receive communication - such as an email - in addition to account area notifications.

Examples from user research:

“I would expect to be notified through the website that the child benefit has been approved, and SMS if enabled.”

from phase 2, round 1

“This is a new notification or new message, an update on a form I filled in previously…I would expect an email.”

from phase 2, round 1
