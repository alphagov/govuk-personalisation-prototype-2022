We engaged users in discussion around reasons for deleting their data, and what the impact of deleting their data would be.

Some users commented that they would delete data if they felt the security of their account had been compromised. In this way, 'delete data' is perceived as a security function.

Some users believed that if they deleted their data they would have to 'start from scratch'.

There may be some confusion around deleting data from a GOV.UK account vs. the perception that service data has been deleted.

This feature would benefit from further user research to explore the impact of deleting data and what that means for data submitted to various services.
