User research methodology is limited in relation to this feature and generally in relation to the testing of whether or not users would click on links. In order to fully explore whether links are effective a multi-variate test with a bigger sample would be required.

Ethics and liability are important considerations for any type of recommendations. As mentioned in the insights section, if users require a service that has been pushed further down a list or page due to a 'recommended' algorithm there could be some liability if important information is missed.
