Risks

  • Red flag with domestic abuse - needs to have safeguards to ensure the account is not compromised
  • The obvious one is the creation and breakup of households, users would need to be able to quickly tell us they're setting up or breaking up a relationship
  • The speed with which people would keep that up to date

Policy considerations:

  • Adequate security controls will need to be put in place (for example use of 2-factor authentication) in order to prevent abuse of data/fraud, data being used in the wrong way or shared with the wrong person
  • As users will be given the choice as to whether or not they share their information with another account it isn't a huge concern
  • Consideration needs to be taken into data items and level of data that can and can't be shared with another account
