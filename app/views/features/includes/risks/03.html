Future design considerations

The GOV.UK account will be the biggest change to GOV.UK since its launch.

In time, every page / department / service will be affected by the account and its features. This will create a set of new design challenges which will stretch the current design system.

! Warning New patterns and components will inevitably be required

Scalability challengs

Our research has enabled us to highlight design and performance scalability challenges, such as:

  • How will the dashboard UI tolerate lots of features, and lots of data being saved to the account?
  • Is the feature still clear and simple to operate? Does it still meet the user needs, such as saving time and reducing cognitive load?
  • How does the account pull back and store 100+ data items? What are the performance impact and challenges?

Ownership

Much like homepages and navigations - dashboards can become unwieldy without proper control.

We believe there will be ownership challenges with the dashboard, with multiple teams having ownership stakes in various components used across the account space.

Clear ownership and recognised, appropriate design patterns will need to be established, to ensure coherency and consistency.
