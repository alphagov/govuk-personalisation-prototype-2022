Risks

One of the risks of connecting external data will be accuracy and the subsequent impact on the user. GOV.UK account could be responsible and liable for external data being handled accurately.

  • Would need to consider the validity of the information, whoever was providing us with the information was doing the same robust checks
  • Concerns around transparency with the end user
  • Government is held to a higher standard than 3rd parties, there's no parity of safeguarding
  • A huge amount of education would need to take place with the end user
  • The long tail effect - users share information because it is convenient but they don't think about the long tail effects

Legal

  • Stepping outside into the private sector is quite a 'serious threshold', in order to do so evidence of necessity and the benefits it will bring need to be provided
  • Understanding where you draw the line, what external sources are and aren't acceptable to source data from?
  • Must be transparent with users about why we are collecting data from external sources and what exactly we are using it for in order for them to be comfortable with us doing so
