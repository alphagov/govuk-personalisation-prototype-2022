How it works

Use this service to get your 30 hours free childcare or pay for your Tax-Free Childcare.

To keep getting your 30 hours free childcare or Tax-Free Childcare, you must confirm your details are up to date every 3 months. You can use your GOV.UK account to do this automatically, saving you time.

If you’re eligible for 30 hours free childcare, sign in to get a code to give to your childcare provider.

If you’ve registered for Tax-Free Childcare, sign in to:

  • pay your childcare provider
  • set up automated payments
  • apply for a new child

You’ll need your GOV.UK account details to sign in to this service.

You sign in to your account in a different way if you’re a childcare provider.
