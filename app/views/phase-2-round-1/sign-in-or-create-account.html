Sign in or create a GOV.UK account

You need a GOV.UK Account to save your progress. If you don’t have a GOV.UK Account, you can create one.

The GOV.UK Account is different to other government accounts you already have (for example your personal tax account or Government Gateway).
More about GOV.UK Accounts

GOV.UK Account is a new account that we’re starting to use for some government services.

Other services use different accounts, such as Government Gateway or your Universal Credit account.

In the future, you’ll be able to use your GOV.UK Account for all government services. For now, you need different accounts for different services.

You’ll need an email address and a UK mobile phone number. It will take no more than 5 minutes.
