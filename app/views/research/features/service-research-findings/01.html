Information a user saves in their account could be used to work out what services they are eligible for. If a user meets the service criteria, the GOV.UK account could suggest that they may be eligible to apply to a particular service.

When logged in, eligibility for certain services/benefits may be suggested to the user.

This feature could eventually be coupled with auto-application.

There's lots to understand around how users react to this, what users expect from it, and how and which data is used to inform suggestions.

There's also the mirror image of dealing with removed eligibility for things, based on data changes.
